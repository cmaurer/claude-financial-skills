## Target-Date Funds (map to asset class)

Target-date funds hold a blend of equity and fixed income that shifts
automatically. For stress testing, decompose by the fund's equity/bond
allocation at the target year — do not apply a single equity shock.

| Approximate Year | Equity % | Bond % |
|-----------------|---------|-------|
| 2055+ | ~90% | ~10% |
| 2040 | ~80% | ~20% |
| 2035 | ~70% | ~30% |
| 2030 | ~60% | ~40% |
| 2025 | ~50% | ~50% |
| Income | ~30% | ~70% |
