## Moat Indicators

- **Switching costs**: enterprise software, payments, ERP systems
- **Network effects**: marketplaces, social platforms, payment rails
- **Cost advantage**: scale manufacturing, proprietary distribution
- **Intangibles**: patents (pharma), brands (consumer staples), licenses
- **Efficient scale**: regulated utilities, niche industrial suppliers
